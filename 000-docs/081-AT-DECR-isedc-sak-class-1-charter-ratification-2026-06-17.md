## 1. Mission of this record

Ratify the Spec Authority Kernel (SAK) — the bicameral expansion of `@intentsolutions/core` that adds a `schemas/authoring/v1/` chamber as the single internal source of truth for "what is a valid agent-native authoring artifact" — at ISEDC Class-1 (immutable-artifact + cross-consumer-ripple) tier. The charter gates ~40 IEP beads. This record preserves the verbatim 7-seat positions and the binding conditions of ratification, so a future reader can reconstruct exactly why ratification landed where it did.

## 2. Why a council, not a single review

The SAK mints immutable artifacts (a distinct predicate-URI namespace, signing trust-root topology) and changes how every downstream consumer (the CCP validator, the 7 internal SKILL.md validators, j-rig) integrates. Its failure modes are asymmetric — a wrong call on the signing root or the CCP flip is measured in stranded signed evidence or broken third-party trust, not a code revert. That asymmetry is what the 7-seat adversarial pattern exists for.

## 3. Synthesis lenses (applied by every seat)

1. **The arena** — 5 surfaces: APIs · CLIs · MCP servers · agents · SKILL.md
2. **Both sides** — client-side + server-side eval
3. **The transformation pipeline** — API→CLI→MCP→SKILL.md→agent
4. **Composable partial attestation** — every component is a valid entry; silence ≠ failure
